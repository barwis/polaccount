## Overview Timeline (2 people team)

**Total: ~6–10 weeks (depending on scope & feedback speed)**

- Week 1–2: Discovery & Strategy
- Week 3: Creative Direction
- Week 4–5: Identity Design
- Week 6–7: Website Design
- Week 8: Collateral & Guidelines
- Week 9–10: Rollout & Launch

---

## Phase 1: Discovery & Strategy

**Goal:** Define what the brand should be before designing anything

- [ ]  Business goals and rebrand objectives
- [ ]  Audience definition
- [ ]  Competitor analysis
- [ ]  Brand audit (current assets)

**Outputs:**

- Brand positioning
- Promise / Proposition / Premise / Persona
- Messaging foundation

---

## Phase 2: Creative Direction

**Goal:** Explore visual directions aligned with strategy

- [ ]  2–3 visual territories (moodboards)
- [ ]  Typography directions
- [ ]  Colour directions
- [ ]  Reference UI / layout styles

**Outputs:**

- Chosen design direction
- Visual rationale

---

## Phase 3: Brand Identity

**Goal:** Create a complete, scalable identity system

- [ ]  Logo (primary, secondary, icon)
- [ ]  Colour palette
- [ ]  Typography system
- [ ]  Visual language (icons, imagery, shapes)
- [ ]  Layout principles

**Outputs:**

- Full identity system
- Real-world mockups (website, social, print)

---

## Phase 4: Website Design

**Goal:** Translate brand into a high-performing digital experience

- [ ]  Sitemap
- [ ]  Wireframes
- [ ]  UI design (desktop + mobile)
- [ ]  Design system / components

**Outputs:**

- Full website design (ready for development)

---

## Phase 5: Collateral & Assets

**Goal:** Ensure consistency across all touchpoints

- [ ]  Business cards
- [ ]  Letterhead
- [ ]  Email signature
- [ ]  Presentation template
- [ ]  Social media templates

---

## Phase 6: Brand Guidelines

**Goal:** Make the brand usable internally

- [ ]  Logo usage rules
- [ ]  Colour and typography
- [ ]  Tone of voice
- [ ]  Application examples

---

## Phase 7: Launch & Rollout

**Goal:** Ensure smooth transition

- [ ]  Asset handover
- [ ]  Rollout checklist
- [ ]  Website launch
- [ ]  Post-launch review
